BALASORE

ROUTE(from KOLKATA):

  • Board a train from Howrah Junction or Sealdah Railway Station in Kolkata to Balasore Railway Station.
    There are several trains running between these stations, including the Duronto Express, Shatabdi Express, and various passenger trains.
  • Upon reaching Balasore Railway Station, you can hire a taxi or take a local bus to Dublagadi Beach.
    The beach is approximately 25-30 kilometers away from Balasore Railway Station, and the journey takes around 30-45 minutes by road.
