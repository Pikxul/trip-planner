ROUTE(from KOLKATA):

  • Kolkata to New Jalpaiguri (NJP):
    • Howrah or Sealdah in Kolkata to New Jalpaiguri (NJP) Railway Station.
    • The journey from Kolkata to NJP takes around 8-10 hours, depending on the train.
  • New Jalpaiguri (NJP) to Darjeeling:
    • Upon reaching NJP Railway Station, you'll need to take a taxi or shared jeep to Siliguri Junction.
    • Alternatively, you can take a shared taxi or bus from Siliguri to Darjeeling.
      The road journey takes around 3-4 hours, depending on road conditions and traffic.
