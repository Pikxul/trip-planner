GHATSHILA

ROUTE(from KOLKATA):

  • Kolkata to Ghatshila via Howrah and Tatanagar Junction
    • From Tatanagar Junction, you can take a local train or a bus to Ghatshila, which is about 45 kilometers away.
  • Kolkata to Ghatshila via Howrah and Purulia Junction
    • From Purulia Junction, you can take a local train or a bus to Ghatshila, which is approximately 140 kilometers away.
