BAKKHALI

ROUTE(from KOLKATA):

  • Board a train from Kolkata to Sealdah Railway Station.
  • From Sealdah, you can take a train to Namkhana Railway Station.
    There are several trains operating on this route, including the Namkhana Local and the Local EMU trains.
  • After reaching Namkhana Railway Station, you need to proceed to the Namkhana Ferry Ghat.
  • At Namkhana Ferry Ghat, board a ferry to Kachuberia Ferry Ghat.
  • From Kachuberia Ferry Ghat, take a bus, taxi, or auto-rickshaw to Bakkhali.
    The distance between Kachuberia Ferry Ghat and Bakkhali is around 3-4 kilometers, and the journey takes approximately 10-15 minutes.
